We are the bridge between the customers of the various shopping outlets in the Accra Mall and their respective service providers

So what does The Phoenix actually do

The Phoenix is a multi-platform application that allows the shopping outlets of the Accra Mall to sell their products to existing and propective customers. The mobile platform allows customers to view, comment and ultimately buy their preffered products from thier favorite shopping outlet.


The purchase is done through secured payment methods including credit card (PayPal & Android Wallet) and Mobile Money, which is gaining much popularity and acceptance now.


The shopping outlet administrators are able to keep tracks of their sales and orders delivered at what time and to whom. This helps to measure profit and loss over a specified period of time. Customers are also able to track in real-time, the progress of their content delivery to their predefined drop-off point.
